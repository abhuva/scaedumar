# Inspect

Inspect is a way to narrow attention.

The world holds more signs than the player can use at once. Inspect helps focus on terrain, water, plants, tracks, or known ground so a decision can be made with less noise.

## Use Inspect When

- a route crosses uncertain ground
- water or plants may be nearby
- tracks suggest local movement
- the map shows signs you have not learned yet
