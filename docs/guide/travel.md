# Travel

Travel is committed only after a route is traced.

Use **PF** to preview nearby movement. The route shows whether the destination is reachable and what kind of cost the terrain may ask from the body.

## What Changes Cost

- Distance spends time.
- Slope can make a short path expensive.
- Wet ground can slow movement.
- Fatigue, thirst, and hunger make future choices narrower.
