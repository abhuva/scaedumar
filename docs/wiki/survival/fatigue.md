## Fatigue

Fatigue is the weight left behind by movement and work.

It rises as time is spent on travel, searching, and other effort. High fatigue makes the next route less forgiving, especially when thirst or hunger are already low.

Rest brings it down, but rest also spends time. Choose the stop before exhaustion chooses it for you.

A tired body turns small mistakes into larger ones.
